About the Artist

Ken Baker is a punk rock artist from the Pacific Northwest. Growing up immersed in skateboarding and BMX culture, his art reflects the vibrant and rebellious spirit of these communities. Apart from his artistic endeavors, Ken is also an accomplished aerospace mechanic, coach, and consultant, bringing a unique blend of technical skill and innovation to his work.

Ken is available for commissions in a variety of artistic mediums. He specializes in paintings, pen and ink illustrations, and high-quality prints. Known for his versatile skills, Ken also offers a unique service of creating custom slap stickers where he brings a raw energy to his commissioned work, skillfully blending it with his technical expertise to deliver impactful and distinctive pieces.
